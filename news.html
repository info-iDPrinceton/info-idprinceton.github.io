Covert intelligence has revealed that they are planning something big, as resources have moved around in unpredictable patterns. As the end of Halloweek draws near, it may be a moment of reckoning for not only the Ghostbusters of iD Princeton, but for the United States, the world, and the intergalactic Alliance...
