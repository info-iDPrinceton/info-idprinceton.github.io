PKE Meter Scan

Currently unavailable can not scan

Campers vote for one instructor to scan during the last vote of each day. The following morning the results of the scan will be shared, revealing if the instructor is a ghost or a human.

Campers cannot scan eliminated (busted) instructors.

Campers are allowed to scan the same instructor twice if they are not eliminated from the game.

If an instructor is scanned, they are revealed as they are at the time. This means that if an instructor that is a human on the day they are scanned is turned into a ghost overnight, they will be revealed as a ghost. The same instructor can still be turned into a ghost later in the week.
